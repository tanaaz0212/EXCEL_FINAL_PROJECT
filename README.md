# Final Sales Dashboard (Excel)

## Overview

The **Final Sales Dashboard** is an advanced and refined Excel dashboard that provides a summarized view of sales performance. It focuses on trends, regional analysis, and category-level insights.

---

## Objectives

* Track monthly sales trends
* Analyze regional performance
* Understand category contribution
* Identify top customers
* Evaluate product demand

---

## Filters (Slicers)

* **Months:** January to December
* **Region:** Central, East, North, South, West
* **Category:** Appliances, Electronics, Furniture

---

## Key Metric

* **Total Amount:** ₹229,192.47

---

## Dashboard Components

### 1. Total Amount by Customer

* Horizontal bar chart showing top customers
* Highlights major contributors to revenue

---

### 2. Category vs Unit Price

* Donut chart showing category distribution
* Electronics has the highest contribution

---

### 3. Monthly Trend

* Line chart showing monthly sales performance
* Helps identify trends and seasonality

---

### 4. Quantity Analysis

* Bar chart showing quantity sold per product
* Identifies high-demand products

---

### 5. Region Distribution

* Pie chart showing contribution by region
* Helps compare regional performance

---

## Insights

* Sales vary across months, indicating seasonal trends
* Electronics category leads in revenue contribution
* Some products consistently show high demand
* Regional performance differs significantly

---

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Pivot Charts
* Slicers

---

## Conclusion

The Final Sales Dashboard provides a clear and summarized view of business performance, enabling better decision-making through visual insights and trend analysis.
